## Use Cases

### 1. Robotics Locomotion Control
Train bipedal robots to walk/jog naturally using human motion patterns.
**Companies:** Boston Dynamics, Unitree, Tesla Optimus

### 2. Animation Retargeting
Transfer human motion to 3D characters in games/films.
**Tools:** Unity, Unreal Engine, Blender

### 3. Sports Analytics
Analyze running form and detect inefficiencies.
**Applications:** Coaching apps, fitness trackers

### 4. Gait Analysis & Healthcare
Detect abnormalities in walking patterns for medical diagnosis.
**Research:** Universities, hospitals

### 5. Autonomous Navigation
Train robots to predict pedestrian movement in crowded areas.
**Companies:** Waymo, Cruise, Nuro
